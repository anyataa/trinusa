Company Profile

Trinusa Network or (TN) is an Indonesian Trading Company based in Jakarta, Indonesia. The company was established in 2008 as export import business through worldwide. They have been started to ship various Indonesian local brands product and goods through several countries in the world specifically in Asia.

Vision

To be a leading and trusted company for connecting trade in Indonesia and International which supported by professional resources and management.

Mision

Our priority is satisfaction of our client we strive to provide highest level of quality control and professionalism
